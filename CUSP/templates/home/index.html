About Us

CUSP is a quickly expanding community built for and by fitness enthusiasts, mindfulness practitioners and eco-aware change-makers. Our groundwork rests on two specific fields-

  • Organising Fitness and Mindfulness sessions
  • Helping Restaurants, Cafes, Food chains Go Green swiftly, since it's a tiresome process for the owners.

We believe fitness should complement mindfulness, and vice versa. Although the gym helps an individual with toned and muscular bodies, it's the mind he/she is actually training; the former being a byproduct, an amazing one none the less! Hence our community is on a journey to bring physical fitness and mindfulness on the same pagevent.first. Our session are from various fields, few examples are Movement Culture, Dance Movement Rejuvenation, Yoga, Sound Bath, etc; all of them however serving the same goal:

Going back with a fitter body and peaked mind!

Did you know? We prefer open-air session on grass due to it's playfulness (sparks creativity) and calming qualities!

Our community is also very aware of the environmental complications and believes that giving back to Mother Nature in anyway, whether small or big, is a plus for our planet, especially now when Earth needs us most. We noticed that many restaurants were reluctant to Go Green due to two main reasons:

  • The high cost of ecologically intelligent products/alternatives.
  • The Process of Change (PoC) is often too tiresome for owners, since they have to approach different sellers for different products.

CUSP has taken a pledge to curate financially efficient sustainable eco-friendly products to exactly solve this particular problem. If your cafe is planning to Go Green, all you'll have to do is give us a budget and we'll plan the PoC for you, making the shift as easy as sneezing with your eyes open! In your PoC, we'll help you Go Green over the next few months, depending on your budget and products needed. From eco-friendly chopsticks and straws to momo-steamers and sporks, we got your back! It's high time the eco-battle breakes financial constrictions and seep to the remotest of cafes and restaurants. Let's take ecological awareness beyond hashtags and tea-talks!!
